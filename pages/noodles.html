How to Make Noodles and Sauce

Lasagna is a classic meal that families all of the world enjoy. Whether you're sharing it with family, or you're taking it to work for you co-workers, you can't go wrong with this hearty dish



Ingredients You'll Need

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Ground Beef
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • More Cheese
  • Italian Sausage
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese

Once you have your ingredients, it's time to begin preparing the meal

  1. To begin, gather all of your ingredients together and set them out. Having an organized space is an essential when it comes to cooking
  2. Begin by placeing your ground beef in a skillet and cooking until brown. You will want to add salt, pepper, and a generous amount of italian seasoning.
  3. In a seperate skillet, go ahead and begin browning your italian suasage.
  4. Once the meat is fully cooked, set aside for later.

    5. If you are trying to make the dish healthier, feel free to drain the grease. If health isn't your goal, then keep that grease for flavor!

  5. Now it's time to prepare the noodles. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Be sure to salt the water
  6. Place the pasta sheets in the pot, until they become soft and begin to float to the top. Remove from water and allow them to cool.
  7. Now here comes the fun part. We will line the baking dish with a singular layer of the pasta sheets. On top of the sheet, we will add a layer of meat, ricotta cheese, your other cheeses, and sauce. Repeat this 4 times.
  8. Bake in the oven at 350 degrees for 45 minutes and enjoy!
